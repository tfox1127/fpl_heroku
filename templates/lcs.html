Last Chance Saloon

  • Runs from GW35 to GW38
  • Bottom 12 in the league as at end of GW34 qualify for Tier 2 Last Chance Saloon (LCS) knockout
  • League positions 15th-18th as at end of GW 34 get a bye to the second round of Tier 2 knockout
  • All other teams in the league that are 75 points or more behind the league leader will qualify for the Tier 1 LCS knockout
  • The number of teams that qualify for Tier 1 LCS will determine the number of teams that get a bye to the second round but byes will be given to the highest ranked teams as at end of GW34
  • Each round is played over 1 GW only
  • The team with the highest FPL score in each round will advance
  • In the event of a tie in the knockout rounds, team(s) eliminated will be determined by
    1. Lowest scoring captain will be eliminated
    2. Lowest scoring vice captain will be eliminated
    3. Highest league position as at that point in time will be eliminated
  • Use of any chip in any week is allowed in LCS and is recommended as a strategy if that is your goal

Draw: Seedings will be driven by league position at the end of GW34 with the higher league position resulting in a higher seed.

Example: For Tier 2 LCS, 15th place will be Seed 1, 16th = Seed 2, 17th = Seed 3 etc.
